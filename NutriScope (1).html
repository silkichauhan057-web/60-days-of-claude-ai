Nutrition Data Sources

📖 Food composition estimates are compiled from typical published ranges found in the USDA FoodData Central database and the Indian Food Composition Tables (IFCT 2017) — used as general reference points, not a live data pull.
🎯 Calorie targets use the Mifflin-St Jeor equation with standard activity multipliers (1.2–1.9).
🧬 Micronutrient targets (RDA) are approximate adult daily values based on commonly published dietary guidelines (e.g. ICMR-NIN / general RDA references) and may vary by country, age, and individual health status.
⚠️ Values are estimates for educational use. For lab-verified nutrition data or personal deficiency diagnosis, consult a registered dietitian or your national food composition database.
