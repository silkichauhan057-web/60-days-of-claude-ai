## Key Learnings
- A single resume can be repurposed into many different outreach formats (cover letter, cold email, LinkedIn message, elevator pitch) without changing the underlying facts — just the framing and length.
- Being explicit about in-progress skills (like Power BI) in outreach content builds more credibility than overstating proficiency, especially before an interview where it would surface anyway.
- A "recruiter headline" forces a level of positioning clarity that a resume alone doesn't — useful for LinkedIn and quick introductions.
- Skill gap analysis is most useful when paired with a concrete roadmap (specific next 2-3 actions), not just a list of missing skills.
- Templates with placeholders (`[Company Name]`, etc.) are more reusable across multiple applications than one-off personalized drafts.
